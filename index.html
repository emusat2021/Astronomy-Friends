ABOUT

Astronomy Friends is a non profit organisation who aims at bringing people in the world of astronomy.
Did you know that with a pair of binoculars or a modest telescope you could see some objects orbiting Earth?
We can help you discover when a specific object will pass your skies.
Are you ready to dive in in the wonderful world of astronomy?

SATELLITES TRACKER

How it works:

1. Enter a satellite name/ID in the Name/ID field.
Choose one from Favorite Objects or start typing a name and choose from suggestions.

2. Click on the map to pick a position or Click on Get my location button.
Latitude and Longitude fields will fill automatically.

Note: allow this page to access location services when choosing Get my location.
